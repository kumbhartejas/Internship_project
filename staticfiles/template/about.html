Our Story

Vintage Bites started with a passion for bringing authentic and timeless recipes to life. Our mission is to serve hearty meals that feel like home, made with the freshest ingredients and a lot of love.

1985 – Humble Beginnings

What started as a 5-table family kitchen became a neighborhood favorite thanks to Nonna Maria’s legendary lasagna.

1995 – Expansion

Our dining hall expanded, live music nights began, and word-of-mouth brought food lovers from all over the city.

Today – A Culinary Legacy

We’ve stayed true to our roots—fresh ingredients, family-style warmth, and timeless recipes passed down generations.
